# Progression Rules

- Keep 1–3 reps in reserve on strength exercises.
    
- When all sets reach the top of the rep range, increase difficulty.
    
- Progress using:
    
    - More weight
        
    - Harder variation
        
    - More range of motion
        
    - Slower lowering
        
    - Pauses
        
- Do not take kettlebell swings to failure.
    

---

# Conditioning Targets

- Rucking: 1–3 times/week
    
- Kettlebell swing conditioning: included on Thursday
    
- Optional extra swing session:

- 10–15 kettlebell swings
